## 1.0.0

Enhancing Video Support
features:

1. `Seamless Video Integration` Embed videos within a slider, creating a fluid multimedia experience for users.
2. `Effortless Video Control` Enable users to manage video playback without exiting the slider, providing uninterrupted engagement.
3. `Dynamic MediaView Descriptions` Implement dynamic descriptions for each MediaView, enhancing content context and user interaction.
